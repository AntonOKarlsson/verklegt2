Financing

You have questions about financing your property purchase?

Don't worry, friend, because you have come to the right place!

At Castle Apartments we are always looking out for you, the customer! And that is why we offer the three most exciting and sensible ways of financing your purchase:

  • Bank Mortgage: Bank mortgages from all the major Icelandic banks can be used to purchase properties at Castle Apartments. You only have to know the number of the mortgage, the amount and the bank. We will take care of the rest.
  • Credit Cards: We accept all major credit cards. You can safely insert your credit card information during the purchase step and the property is yours!
  • Bank transfer: If you want to purchase your property using a bank transfer, no problem! You can find our payment information when you go through the purchase process on the website. Remember to add the property number as an explanation!
